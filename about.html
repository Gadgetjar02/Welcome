Who We Are

Ohanaeze Ndigbo Worldwide is the apex Igbo socio-cultural organization dedicated to uniting the Igbo people globally and fostering unity, peace and socio-economic development for the good of all.

Founded on the foundation of unity, social charity, fairness to the Ndigbo race and integrity.

Our Mission

Promoting unity, culture, justice and cultural heritage, while advancing economic empowerment and political participation among Igbo people.

Our Vision

For social justice, economic thrive and cultural preservation of Ndigbo and everyone.

Our Core Values

Unity

Togetherness over diversity always.

Heritage

Preserve our cultural roots and values.

Progress

Development in every generation.

Integrity

Upholding honesty in leadership.

Strength

Leadership firmness in service to all.

Our Objectives

  • Promote unity among all Ndigbo irrespective of villages, politics or social status.
  • Preserve and promote the history, languages and culture.
  • Represent the socio-political aspirations of Ndigbo.
  • Champion the rights and welfare of Ndigbo.
  • Raise awareness of our challenges both local and global.
  • Foster cooperation with government and global goals.

Together, we can achieve more
