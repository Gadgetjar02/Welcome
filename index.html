Who We Are

Uniting Igbo People Across Oyo State

Ohanaeze Ndi Igbo Worldwide, Oyo State Chapter, is the official body representing the interests of Igbo indigenes residing in Oyo State. We are a bridge between our local community and the global Ohanaeze movement.

From advocating for members' welfare to preserving Igbo culture and fostering economic empowerment — our mission is holistic and enduring.

Scholarships

Available Scholarships

🏆 Ohanaeze Oyo State Annual Bursary Award 2025

Open to all registered Igbo students in accredited tertiary institutions within Oyo State. Minimum 2.0 GPA required. Applicant must be a child or ward of a registered association member.

⏳ Application Deadline: June 30, 2025

🎯 Igbo Professionals Foundation Scholarship

For final-year undergraduate students in Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM) fields. Award covers tuition and a monthly stipend for one academic session.

⏳ Application Deadline: July 15, 2025

📖 Ndi Igbo Cultural Excellence Award

Awarded to students who demonstrate outstanding achievement in Igbo language, culture, and community service. Open to students in any discipline.

⏳ Application Deadline: August 1, 2025
